1. Movie Theatre Revenue & Audience Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

The **Movie Theatre Revenue & Audience Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI**. It analyzes movie theatre performance by transforming raw movie data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and visualizations.

The project consists of **two dashboards**:
- **Movie Analysis Dashboard** – Focuses on financial performance and revenue analysis.
- **Audience Analysis Dashboard** – Focuses on audience engagement, ratings, and review metrics.

2.Business Problem

Movie theatres generate large volumes of data related to movie performance, revenue, ratings, and audience engagement. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify profitable movies, understand audience preferences, compare market performance, and optimize business strategies.
This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing an interactive Business Intelligence solution that converts raw data into actionable insights.

3.Goal of the Dashboard

- Analyze movie revenue and profitability.
- Monitor overall theatre performance using KPIs.
- Identify top-performing movies and countries.
- Analyze audience ratings and voting trends.
- Compare revenue across different years and regions.
- Support business decisions through interactive visualizations.

4.Dashboard Details

## Dashboard 1 – Movie Analysis

The Movie Analysis Dashboard provides a complete overview of financial performance and movie success.

### KPI Cards
- Total Movies
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit

### Interactive Filter
- Month Slicer

### Visualizations
- Movie Performance Overview (Matrix Table)
- Displays movie details with **Rating > 6**
- Revenue by Year
- Revenue by Rating
- Revenue by Country

### Business Insights
- Monitors overall business performance.
- Identifies high-performing movies.
- Tracks yearly revenue growth.
- Compares revenue across countries.
- Analyzes the impact of ratings on revenue.

## Dashboard 2 – Audience Analysis

The Audience Analysis Dashboard focuses on customer engagement and movie popularity.

### KPI Cards

- Total IMDb Votes

- Average Rotten Tomatoes Score

### Visualizations
- Rating by Total Voting
- Total Rotten Tomatoes Votes by Country
- Rotten Tomatoes Score by Average Rating

### Business Insights
- Measures audience engagement.
- Compares IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes voting trends.
- Evaluates audience ratings and critic scores.
- Identifies countries with highly rated movies.
